Discover Our Story

Born out of Fung Suk Wangdu's creation, YumFusion pays homage to his passion for Nepali cuisine. Imaginative yet rooted in tradition, Wangdu’s cuisine showcases the vibrant essence of his cooking, with strong influences from both traditional and local ingredients. Using time-honoured classic techniques and modern methods, our dishes showcase the regionally specific spices and flavours of the South Asian seaboard.

Our goal is simple: Make real Nepali food; recreate the exact experience we had back in Nepal. The results? Better than we ever imagined. Flattering reviews from customers and the local media have led to a family of restaurants. Along with our stylish design, warm character, and lively tunes, we promote a “live to eat” ethos and foster a convivial spirit at the table. Above all, YumFusion celebrates authentic, flavourful food and drink enjoyed amongst old and new friends. Blessed with a talented and hard-working team who share that passion to make great food, we invite you to experience a taste of Nepal.
